Way for Direct Tax Payments
e-Pay Tax Service
As per the advice from Income Tax Department (ITD), ICICI Bank migrated Direct Tax payments to e-Pay Tax service from Nov 01, 2022.
Features
Make direct online tax payments with ICICI Bank’s E-Pay Tax Service!
Features and Benefits:
Pay Direct Tax, Indirect Tax, GST, Custom Duty, State Tax, e-freight, EPF and ESI
View previous and current challans
Use the Bulk Upload Option for minor codes of 100, 200 and 300.
Eligibility:
You can use the E-Pay Tax service and make payments through Net Banking, ICICI Debit Card or at an ICICI Bank counter.
Application Process:
The facility is available to all ICICI Bank customers. Follow the steps below:
Visit ‘
https://eportal.incometax.gov.in /iec/foservices/#/e-pay-tax-prelogin
’
Fill in the details
Choose ICICI Bank and make your payments easily.
Form and List of Documents
ICICI Bank makes it easier for you to make e-tax payments, for a variety of different taxes. You need the following documents to make the payments:
Aadhaar and PAN Card
Form-16
Salary Slips
Interest Certificates from Post Offices and Banks
Tax-Saving Investment Proofs
Proofs of Capital Gains
Health Insurance Premium Receipts.
Now pay Direct Taxes in the following ways:
1. Pay through
https://eportal.incometax.gov.in /iec/foservices/#/e-pay-tax-prelogin
> fill details, select ICICI Bank and Pay. Challan will be generated from e-Pay Tax services at e-Filing Portal only
2. For ‘Pay at Bank Counter’ transaction- Visit
https://eportal.incometax.gov.in /iec/foservices/#/e-pay-tax-prelogin
> fill details> Pay at Bank Counter > Select ICICI Bank and visit any of the ICICI Bank Branch
3. For TDS on sale/rent of property/payment to resident contractors and professionals,
https://eportal.incometax.gov.in/iec /foservices/#/login
> Login with PAN > e-pay Tax > Select 26QB/26QC/26QD
(Buyer and Seller details can be auto populated basis "Copy" feature)
Please Note:
a) Challan will be available only in e-Pay Tax services on the e-Filing Portal: Log in to
https://eportal.incometax.gov.in /iec/foservices/#/login
> Payment history > Download Challan.
b) Debit Advice for payment done will be available in Corporate Banking > Tax> Tax Payments > Direct tax > Tax Payment inquiry > Download

What are the payment modes available for Direct Tax payments on e-pay Tax Service at the e-Filing Portal through ICICI Bank?
The payment modes below will be available on e-Pay tax service at the e-Filing Portal for Direct Tax Payments:
Net Banking
ICICI Bank Debit Card
Pay at the Bank Counter through 5000+ ICICI Bank Branches.
How can a customer login to e-pay Tax Service at the e-Filing Portal for initiating Direct Tax transactions? How will the customer proceed if the transaction is initiated on NSDL and is unable to find ICICI Bank?
The customer will have to visit the e-Pay tax service at the e-Filing Income Tax Portal using the link
https://www.incometax.gov.in/iec/foportal/
e-pay Tax Service at the e-Filing Portal has a pre-login section which can be accessed by the tax payer, by entering their PAN/TAN along with the mobile number and OTP. Through this section, few types of Direct Tax transactions can be initiated. Link for pre-login section:
https://eportal.incometax.gov.in/iec/foservices/#/e-pay-tax-prelogin/user-details
Additionally, e-pay Tax Service at the e-Filing Portal also has a logged in section, which can be accessed by the taxpayer, by entering their PAN/TAN and password. Through this section, all types of Direct Tax transactions can be initiated. Link for login section: https://eportal.incometax.gov.in/iec/foservices/#/login
Can a customer view the previous challan on ICICI Bank Internet Banking?
Yes, a customer can view all the challans paid until Oct 31, 2022. With effect from Nov 01, 2022, the customer will only receive debit advice from the Internet Banking portal. For challan download, the customer needs to visit the e-Filing portal.
Which digital channels will be available to a corporate customer for Direct Tax payments under e-pay Tax Service at the e-Filing Portal through ICICI Bank?
The channels below will be available to a corporate customer for Direct Tax payments under e-pay Tax Service at the e-Filing Portal:
Net Banking through e-pay Tax Service at the e-Filing Portal
Bulk Upload Option (minor codes 100, 200 and 300 only)
ICICI Bank Debit Card through e-pay Tax Service at the e-Filing Portal.
Through which authorised Branch of ICICI Bank can a customer make payments for Direct Tax transactions under the “Pay at Bank Counter” in e-pay Tax Service at the e-Filing Portal and under which payment modes?
The customer can make payments at any of the ICICI Bank Branches through cash, cheque or DD under the “Pay at Bank Counter” option available on e-pay Tax Service at the e-Filing Portal for a Direct Tax transaction.
