What is an Education Loan?
An Educational Loan assists students in funding their studies by covering expenses such as tuition fees, books and other educational costs.
Who can apply for an Education Loan?
Students who have secured admission in National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) accredited Universities/Institutions, National Board of Accreditation (NBA) recognised professional courses, Centrally Funded Technical Institutes or all other recognised International Universities/Institutions can apply for an Education Loan.
How can I apply for an Education Loan?
You can apply through our website or you can visit any ICICI Bank branch or apply through the VidyaLakshmi portal
link
.
What is the interest rate on an Education Loan?
Interest rates vary based on the type of loan (Secured/Unsecured), Applicant/Co-applicant’s profile, Institute/College category and type of course.
What is an EMI Calculator?
An
EMI Calculator
helps estimate payments and effectively plan finances. It serves as a support system for students looking to pursue higher education in India or abroad.
When does loan repayment start?
You can start as late as only after your course is over. Moreover, we provide flexibility in repayment during your course period as well.
What is the repayment tenure?
Repayment tenure is up to 15 years (including the course period), depending on the loan amount and terms.
Can I pre-pay or pre-close the loan? Are there any charges?
Yes, you can pre-pay or pre-close your loan any time during the tenure. There are no pre-payment or foreclosure charges.
Who is considered eligible as the Co-applicant?
Parents, Siblings, Spouse, Father-in-law, Mother-in-law, Daughter-in-law, Maternal and Paternal Uncle/Aunt and Grandparents are eligible to be your Co-applicant.
Is collateral required for an Education Loan and what can be provided as collateral?
Collateral will be required for Secured Loans only. Collaterals accepted as security are Fixed Deposits, Property: Residential/Commercial or an existing Home/Mortgage Loan with ICICI Bank.
