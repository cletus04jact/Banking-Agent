FX-Retail
Reserve Bank of India (RBI), through Clearing Corporation of India Ltd (CCIL), has rolled out an electronic trading platform ‘FX-Retail’. Now customers have the option to BUY and SELL foreign exchange through FX Retail platform, currently offering Cash/Tom/Spot deals in USD/INR.
For registration, please visit CCIL-FX Retail website,
https://www.fxretail.co.in/#/login
Post successful registration, the limit can be setup through Internet Banking, Phone Banking or by visiting the nearest branch of ICICI Bank.
For more information, visit the CCIL website,
https://www.ccilindia.com/AboutUs/Pages /FxRetailPlatform.aspx
For outward remittances, ICICI Bank currently processes Cash/Tom deals.

How can I buy ICICI Bank Forex Services online?
To buy ICICI Bank Forex Services online, log in to your ICICI Bank Internet Banking Account > Go to ‘Forex Services’ > Select 'Buy Forex’, choose the desired currency and amount, provide the required details such as travel dates and complete the transaction by making the payment through available options.
What currencies can I buy online through ICICI Bank?
ICICI Bank offers a variety of currencies like USD, EUR, GBP, AUD, CAD, SGD and JPY. The exact list of available currencies can be checked on the ICICI Bank Forex Services page.
Are there any fees or charges for buying the Forex Services online?
Yes, ICICI Bank charges fees for buying the Forex Services online. These include a basic service fee, Goods and Services Tax (GST) and possibly other charges depending on the currency and amount. Detailed information about the fees can be viewed during the transaction process or found on the Bank’s official website under the ‘Forex Services’ section.
Can I cancel or modify my Forex order after placing it online?
Once a Forex order is placed online, it generally cannot be cancelled or modified through the online platform. However, you can contact ICICI Bank’s Customer Service immediately to enquire about the potential options available for cancellation or modification. They will assist you depending on the status of your order.
What types of accounts can I open with an online Forex Service?
You can open various accounts with an online Forex Service such as Personal Forex Accounts for travel, Student Accounts for studying abroad and Business Accounts for international transactions. These accounts let you conveniently manage foreign currency, ensuring seamless payments and currency exchanges worldwide.
How does Foreign Exchange (Forex) work?
You may purchase and sell foreign currency using ICICI Bank Forex Services. At a predetermined rate, you exchange your native currency for the foreign currency of the country you are visiting. With this concept, you can effortlessly spend or withdraw money abroad.
Can I access online Forex Services from my mobile device?
Yes, you can access online Forex Services from your mobile device through the ICICI Bank website or iMobile app. These platforms allow you to conveniently buy, sell and manage Foreign Exchange transactions, check real-time rates and load your Multi-currency Card, all from your mobile device.
How secure are online Forex Services?
Online Forex Services at ICICI Bank are highly secure, with encryption, two-factor authentication and other safety measures. These features protect your personal and financial data, ensuring safe transactions for Foreign Currency Exchange and Card Management.
