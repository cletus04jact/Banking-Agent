Bill Payment Using ICICI Bank's Digital Channels
Experience a smarter, simpler way of paying bills online with ICICI Bank’s digital channels. ICICI Bank offers multiple channels for online bill payments such as iMobile, Internet Banking, InstaBIZ & Pockets. With over 21,000 registered billers, these channels help you manage all your bill payments with ease. Payments can be made through multiple payment options, i.e. Savings Account, Credit Cards, PayLater, etc.
You also get the added benefit of multiple and timely reminders to ensure that you never miss any payment due dates. On paying through ICICI Bank’s digital modes, bills get auto-registered for ‘Proactive Instruction Fetch’ from the subsequent month onwards. You can also use the Auto Pay functionality with a limit amount, so that bills up to a certain amount get paid automatically. For Early Pay billers, you can also set Auto Pay on the Early Pay date to get a discount every month.
Why should I choose ICICI Bank for Bill Payment?
Instant bill registration
Register your biller quickly by entering your Customer ID and fetch your bill details instantly.
Bill generation notifications
Stay informed with monthly notifications as soon as your bill is generated. This ensures that you never miss a bill payment.
Instant payment confirmation
Get instant payment confirmation once the amount is debited from your Account, providing a hassle-free experience.

How can I search for a biller?
You can search for a biller by typing the biller name in the Universal Search Bar available in the Bill Pay section on iMobile. You can also navigate to the biller category and select the biller from the list of billers.
I am not able to view my biller. Why is this happening?
Given below are possible reasons for a biller not being found:
The biller is not onboarded on Bharat Connect.
The biller is newly added to Bharat Connect and not updated at ICICI Bank’s end. In this case, please try again after a few days.
The biller is not onboarded with ICICI Bank.
What is the convenience fee charged for paying a bill?
ICICI Bank doesn’t charge any convenience fee for paying a bill or making a recharge.
Can I check details of my previous bill payments??
You can view the bill payment history for the last 15 days under the ‘Bill Payments History
’
tab. You can use filters like biller, category, date, date range, etc. to view specific bill details.
How often does iMobile send reminders for payment of due bills?
iMobile sends SMS to remind you of due bills at these stages:
When the bill is generated
7 days before due date
3 days before due date
On the due date
Where can I check my registered billers?
You can check all registered bills by clicking on ‘View My Bills’ in the Bill Pay section.
When will I know that a bill has been paid successfully?
You will be shown if your bill payment was successful or not on the final screen of the bill payment process. A successful payment will get reflected at the biller’s end immediately. An SMS alert on successful payment is also sent to your registered mobile number.
